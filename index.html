Bio

  • I am a Ph.D. Candidate under Asst. Prof Xia Kelin's research group in the Division of Mathematical Sciences, School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences at Nanyang Technological University. I previously completed my B.Sc. (with Honours) in Mathematical Sciences (with specialization in Applied Mathematics) at NTU, where his honours dissertation was completed under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Le Hai Khoi.
