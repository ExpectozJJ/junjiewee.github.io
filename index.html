Bio

  • JunJie Wee is a Ph.D. Candidate under Asst. Prof Xia Kelin's research group in the Division of Mathematical Sciences, School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences at Nanyang Technological University. Before, JunJie completed his B.Sc. (with Honours) in Mathematical Sciences (with specialization in Applied Mathematics) at NTU. His honours dissertation was completed under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Le Hai Khoi.


Research Interests

    Applied Topology/Geometry in Molecular Sciences:

  • Topology-based machine learning in molecular sciences, material sciences
  • Discrete Geometry-based data analysis and machine learning

    • Complex Analysis and Operator Theory:

  • Korenblum Maximum Principle
  • Operators acting on spaces of holomorphic or entire functions
